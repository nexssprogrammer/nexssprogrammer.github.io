Open Source Research Engineer - Intern


FULL/PART TIME, REMOTE

Preferred Qualifications

  • Research-minded, ability to run experiments independently and present results to leadership,
    • at least one of:
    • Machine Learning,
    • Pattern Recognition,
    • Natural Language Processing,
    • Text Mining,
    • Information Extraction,
    • Recommender Systems,
    • Text Classification,
    • Computer Vision,
    • Reinforcement Learning,
    • Neural Networks,
    • Distributed Systems,
    • Large Scale Systems,
    • Distributed Algorithms,
    • Kernel Distributed Storage,
    • Distributed Databases,
    • Parallel Computing.

Nice To Have

  • Java
    • C++

Benefits

  • Develop innovative tools and applications,
    • Learn, build new/innovative technologies or services based on business demands and industry trends.
Intern Information

An internship with Nexss.com lasts 2-12 months. There is no financial compensation provided during the internship, but if you will match with upcoming projects we will offer you a position after your internship.

NOTE: We will try to answer for all your emails, but we can't promise that we will - it depends on the number of submissions.

If you are interested in any of the positions please send to us your CV (with some examples of your work) at: mapoart@gmail.com

Spontaneous application

We are always looking for great talents, so if you don't see any position which match with your qualifications please send us a CV and we will contact you. mapoart@gmail.com
