Introduction

Welcome to the Nexss Programmer Blog. Here you will find more information about how to use the Nexss Programmer.

Nexss Programmer is for people who are starting their career in programming, but also for the professionals. You can also learn about older programming languages that are not so popular anymore. Nexss has many tools and a lot of functionalities to help you gather as much knowledge as possible to become better programmer. It also allow to compose many languages and has a lot of innovative functionality.

Now 51 programming languages are implemented in the Free/Open Source Nexss Programmer.

We will be adding here tutorials, practical examples and much more.
